Fact sheet

Sahara Arena at a glance

All figures from the venue technical rider. Please use the approved positioning: "Kuwait's premier indoor multipurpose arena."

Venue: Sahara Arena, an indoor multipurpose arena at Sahara Kuwait Resort, Sabhan, Kuwait — on the 6th Ring Road, approximately six minutes from Kuwait International Airport.

Developer: Al Jazeera Real Estate Development.

Technical highlights: full-scale rigging grid · centre-hung four-sided LED cube · installed Bose Professional ArenaMatch audio · dedicated show power · broadcast-ready infrastructure · VIP suites, cabins and a private lounge. Detailed technical specifications are shared with organisers and media on request.

Website imagery is architectural concept visualisation unless marked otherwise; confirm usage with the media team before publication.
