Capabilities

Specified for world tours, sized for Kuwait

Figures below are from the venue technical rider. The full rider — rigging plots, power schedules and floor plans — is available to organisers on request.

Production

Hang it, power it, light it

A 55m × 50m structural mother grid on a 5m module carries 250 tonnes across 112 motor points. Stages scale to 50m × 20m with 15m wings. Installed Bose Professional ArenaMatch audio on a Dante network, a centre-hung four-sided LED cube, and 800A of dedicated show power with a 1,600 kVA standby generator.

Logistics

Load in at midnight, doors by evening

A 40m × 60m loading area with two truck bays and 5m × 4m doors gives direct ground-level access to the arena floor — rated to 30 tonnes/m², available for 24-hour load-in and load-out. Backstage: eight dressing rooms, a star dressing room, three green rooms and a production office.

Hospitality & broadcast

Host your guests, feed your feed

Two private suites, twelve VIP cabins and a 180 m² VIP lounge with its own entrance for 150 guests, plus three meeting rooms. Permanent camera positions, a commentary booth, six-core fibre and streaming capability make every event broadcast-ready.
