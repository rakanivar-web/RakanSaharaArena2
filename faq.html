FAQ

Questions, answered

When does Sahara Arena open?

Sahara Arena opens in September 2026 at Sahara Kuwait Resort, Sabhan. The opening programme is announced ahead of opening — register your interest on the Contact page to hear first.

Where is the arena and how do I get there?

At Sahara Kuwait Resort in Sabhan, on the 6th Ring Road — approximately six minutes from Kuwait International Airport and ten minutes from Kuwait City. See Plan Your Visit for directions and parking.

How do I buy tickets?

Tickets are sold exclusively through an official ticketing partner, announced together with the opening programme. Every confirmed event on this site links directly to the official sale — no other seller is authorised.

Is there parking?

Yes — parking for up to 2,000 vehicles on site, including around 250 VIP spaces and valet service. Accessible bays sit closest to the entrance.

What accessibility services are available?

Accessible parking near the entrance, accessible restrooms, elevator access and dedicated wheelchair positions with companion seating. Contact us ahead of your visit for tailored assistance.

What time do doors open?

Door times and entry policies are set per event and published with each event announcement and on your ticket.

Can I hire the arena for my event?

Yes — concerts, championships, exhibitions, corporate events, weddings and ceremonies. Start on the Host an Event page: the venue-hire enquiry goes straight to the commercial team.

I am a journalist — who do I talk to?

The Media Centre has approved brand assets and the press contact route. Media enquiries are prioritised through the contact form with the "Media & press" enquiry type.
