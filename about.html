About Us

Kuwait's next-generation arena

Opening September 2026 at Sahara Kuwait Resort in Sabhan, Sahara Arena is Kuwait's premier indoor multipurpose arena — developed by Al Jazeera Real Estate Development as a premium, future-ready stage for regional and global audiences.

Vision & mission

Designed to lead

Our vision: give Kuwait a stage the region talks about. Our mission: fill it — world-class concerts, sporting spectacles, cultural shows, exhibitions and large-scale corporate events, delivered year-round on a platform engineered to attract regional and global audiences.

ConcertSportEquestrianWeddingExhibition
National infrastructure

More than a venue

Every aspect has been designed with leadership in mind — from spatial flow and acoustics to guest services and premium finishes. Sahara Arena is a national entertainment infrastructure asset supporting Kuwait's tourism, cultural vision and economic diversification ambitions.

Project overview

Engineered from the first row to the last

A centre-hung LED cube, a 112-point rigging grid, broadcast-ready infrastructure and optimised visibility from every seat — with operational logistics engineered for promoters and production teams, and two private suites, twelve VIP cabins and a 180 m² VIP lounge above the action.

The experience

From the first light to the last encore

A night here starts before the show — dinner against a desert horizon, a walk through the colonnades — and ends long after it, in lounges that stay warm when the stage goes dark. Every detail is tuned to the audience, not the machinery behind it.

Company background

Al Jazeera Real Estate Development

Founded in 2002, AJRE is one of Kuwait's leading real-estate developers — recognized for creating innovative destinations and fully integrated property solutions. With expertise spanning development, operations, marketing and facility management, AJRE delivers long-term value through design excellence and operational strength.
