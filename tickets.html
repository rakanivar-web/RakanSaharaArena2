Tickets

On sale with the opening programme

Tickets for every Sahara Arena event will be sold exclusively through an official ticketing partner — announced together with the opening programme. No other seller is authorised.

How it works

Real events, real tickets, one official partner

When an event is confirmed, its page on this site links directly to the official sale. Prices, seat maps and availability are always shown live from the ticketing partner — never estimated here.

The bowl

A bowl that reshapes for every event

Fixed tiers, retractable seating and VIP places across private suites and cabins — reconfigured per event, from full-standing shows to seated cultural nights.

Seating layouts shown at purchase always come from the official ticketing partner for that event.
